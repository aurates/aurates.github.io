## The Art of Meowing

Cats have over 100 different vocalizations. Meowing is reserved mostly for communicating with humans!

### Types of Meows

- **Short meow**: Standard greeting
- **Multiple meows**: Excited to see you
- **Mid-pitch meow**: Asking for something
- **Long, drawn-out meow**: Demand for attention
